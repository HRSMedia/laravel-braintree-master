SCHEDULE A – SERVICE SPECIFICATIONS

  1. STANDARD OUTBOUND TERMINATION – SERVICE DESCRIPTION.  Voyyp.com will provide call termination services to deliver voice traffic from an End User’s originating equipment to the Customer’s network to connect the Customer Interconnection Point to the owner of the dialed number (“Standard Outbound Services”).  All call recording for billing purposes will stop when the call is completed/terminated.
  1. Local Traffic.  Standard Outbound Services includes the termination of calls which originate and terminate in the same local calling area based on the “calling number” and the “called number” (“Local Traffic”).  Voyyp.com will calculate all minute-of-use based Rates on six (6) second minimums with six (6) second increments.  
  2. Long Distance Traffic.  Standard Outbound Services includes the termination to the 50 United States of America (i) intrastate calls, which originate and terminate in different local calling areas within the same state; and (ii) interstate calls, which originate in one state and terminate in another state (collectively “Long Distance Traffic”).  Voyyp.com will calculate all minute-of-use based Rates on six (6) second minimums with six (6) second increments.  International originating Automatic Number Identification (“ANI”), including, but not limited to, a telephone number outside of the 50 United States and the District of Columbia will be rated as indeterminate traffic.
  3. Indeterminate Jurisdictional Calls.  For the purpose of determining each call’s jurisdiction, Voyyp.com will evaluate the originating and terminating information present in the call stream, including, but not limited to, any calling party number and originating or terminating ANI.  If calls presented to Voyyp.com are: (i) without valid 10-digit USA originating ANI as set forth in the US 50 LERG at the time of the call, or (ii) with a 10-digit originating ANI with NPAs of 976, 911, 900, 888, 877, 866, 800 or 700, Voyyp.com will treat the call as being of “Indeterminate Jurisdiction,” and rate and bill such calls at the “then-current” intrastate Rates quoted in the most recent Rate Notification and in effect at the time of the call.  By way of example, the items listed below meet the criteria of “Indeterminate Jurisdiction” as defined above.
  • Blank ANI / No ANI
  • Unrecognizable originating ANI, including, but not limited to, those that are not valid in the US 50 LERG.
  • Toll Free ANI, including, but not limited to, beginning with 800, 866, 877, 888
  • Origination or terminated from or to Canada. From or to any non US 50 states.
  1. Location Routing Number Billing.  Voyyp.com will rate and bill its Services based on Location Routing Number (“LRN”) Rating Methodology.  Voyyp.com will derive the Rate used to bill any given call from the “then-current” Rate Notification as defined above, which will apply based on the respective call’s terminating LRN, when available.  When LRN is not available, Voyyp.com will derive the Rating Methodology used to bill any given call from the “then-current” Rate Notification, which will apply based on the respective call’s Dialed Number. US 50 will be dipped.
